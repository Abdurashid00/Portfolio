NIKE TIFFANY

From New York to the world, timeless perfection looks good anywhere—from 57th to 125th, and beyond. Just as Tiffany & Co. is an iconic NYC brand, the beloved AF-1 silhouette—and its Triple Black colourway—was shaped by its time in NYC. Crafted with elevated touches like premium leather and finished with luxury detailing like the sterling silver piece on the heel, the AF-1 x Tiffany & Co. '1837' brings you instantly recognisable elegance and sophistication. The resulting collaboration represents New York itself and the bridge between basketball and street style that made the AF-1 the crossover icon it is today.
